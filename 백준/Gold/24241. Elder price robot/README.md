### 문제 설명

<p>The idea behind your latest business adventure, BarGain Overview (BGO), is to collect the history of prices for a certain item that is available for sale on the web. The <em>BarGain score</em> of a particular day is defined as the number of days since the price was lower or equal to today's price. You want to report the BarGain score to your customers to help them identify a good buy.</p>
